Know who we are

South Himalaya Consultant has been built for all sizes of business, no matter what size of business you have this is your perfect.

South Himalaya is a leading sustainable energy innovative solution provider company and having capability in providing sustainable energy analysis and consultancy is sought by leading companies and organizations.

As part of our services offer, we can provide a complete analysis of the energy needs of the project, and a full spectrum of analyses of the economic and carbon benefits of different renewable energy options.

We are a well established Power / Energy Consulting company in Tamil Nadu.We have contracts with many Private Power producers across India, where the source of power is Thermal, Biomass, Wind, Co-Gen and Solar Farms.We help companies to sell their power.

We have a large customer base in Tamil Nadu and we work closely with large power trading companies across the country and also various power exchanges. We also specialize in renewable energy power (Solar & Wind). We represent a few very established companies that specialize in setting up and management of Wind and Solar Power Farms.

We are the one-stop solution to Private Power producers and Power Consumers.
